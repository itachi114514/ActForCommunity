At ActforCommunity, we take pride in the meaningful projects we've undertaken to support those in need and uplift our community.

Our Projects

Donation Drive for Batken Conflict Displacement In response to the conflict displacement in the Batken region, we organized a donation drive to collect essential items such as clothes, hygiene products, toys, and food. Thanks to the generosity of our community, we successfully delivered these supplies to a village that accommodated around 40 displaced individuals, providing them with much-needed relief.

Charity Book Drive Our commitment to education led us to oversee a charity book drive that collected books for the regional childrenâ€™s library in Kara-Balta and the regional school in Novo-Nikolaevka village. These books have enriched the lives of countless children, fostering a love for reading and learning.

Charity Fair for Batken Infrastructure Restoration To support the restoration of infrastructure in the Batken region, we organized a charity fair. The funds raised through this event were dedicated to rebuilding and improving the lives of those affected by the conflict.

Sports and Esports Tournaments We believe in the power of sports to bring people together for a good cause. Over the years, we've organized several sports charity tournaments:

Volleyball Tournament: All proceeds went to an organization supporting women with cancer.
Basketball Tournament: The proceeds benefited the Mercy Center, a charity organization providing essential services to those in need.
FIFA Tournament: Funds raised were used to support a long-term social project for women in distress.

Charity Spelling Bee and Art Auction Our club also hosted a charity spelling bee at our school, as well as a charity art auction. Both events were successful in raising funds for various charitable causes while engaging the community in fun and educational activities.

Social Project: Importance of Volunteering One of our latest initiatives involved a social project where our volunteers visited various schools to give insightful presentations on the advantages of volunteering. We aimed to inspire students to get involved in their communities and introduced them to volunteering clubs around Bishkek that they may be interested in joining.
